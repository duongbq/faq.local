iPhone/iPad/iPod Touch

Last Updated: Feb 07, 2013 03:26PM PST

Note: This article only applies to users who have downloaded an App prior to its removal from the Apple App Store.
Make sure you are running iOS version 4.3.3 or higher before attempting these steps.

  1. Tap the "App Store" icon.
  2. Tap the "Updates" button.
  3. Tap the "Purchased" button.
